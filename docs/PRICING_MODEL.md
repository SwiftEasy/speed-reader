# SpeedReader Pricing Model

## Tiered Pricing Strategy

### Free Tier - $0
**Target:** Try-before-buy users
- TXT files only
- 400 WPM max
- No save progress
- Basic chapter detection
- Watermark/branding

### Basic Tier - $3/month or $25/year
**Target:** Students, casual readers
- PDF/EPUB/TXT support
- Chapter navigation
- Save reading progress
- 800 WPM max
- 2-3 device limit

### Pro Tier - $6/month or $50/year
**Target:** Power readers, professionals
- All Basic features
- AI-powered chapter detection
- Unlimited WPM
- Highlights & notes
- Study mode (flashcards from content)
- Priority support

### Lifetime - $49 one-time
**Target:** Users who hate subscriptions
- All Pro features forever
- Early access to new features
- ~8 months of Pro = break-even

## Differentiators

1. **AI-powered chapter navigation** - Unique feature
2. **Modern UI** - Most competitors look dated
3. **PDF support with structure** - Many only do TXT/EPUB
4. **Web-based** - No install, works everywhere
5. **Science/technical content modes** - Future roadmap

## Notes

- Students share accounts - consider embracing viral growth over strict limits early on
- $3/mo is "coffee price" - low friction for students
- Annual pricing gives 2 months free incentive
- Lifetime captures price-sensitive users who hate subscriptions
